Cake

Ingredients:

1 tin Nestlé MILKMAID 1 (25g) Nestlé Milk Chocolate 100g Butter 1¾ cup (175 g) Maida3-4 heaped tbsp (25g) Cocoa 1 tspBaking Powder 1 tsp Soda Bicarbonate 1 tsp Vanilla Essence 1-1/3 cups (200ml) Aerated soda 1 small tin Tinned Cherries 200g Cream 2 tsp Icing Sugar/Powdered Sugar

How to Make Mutton Biryani:

Step 1: Grease an 8” diameter baking tin and dust it with flour. Step 2: Pre heat the oven at 180o C. Step 3: Sieve together maida, cocoa powder, baking powder and soda bicarbonate (sieve 2-3 times for better aeration). Step 4: Soften butter (DO NOT MELT). Add Nestlé MILKMAID and beat well. Add the vanilla essence. Step 5: Add maida mixture and aerated soda alternately to the batter till all the maida and soda are used up. Step 6: Pour the batter into the baking tin (8” diameter) and bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes or till the toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean. Step 7: Remove from oven, cool for a while. Loosen sides of cake, using a knife if necessary. Turnout over a wire rack/plate lined with cloth (to absorb moisture) and cool slightly before cutting horizontally into 2. Step 8: Drain & destone cherries. Soak both cakes halves with cherry syrup. Step 9: Whip cream and sugar till light and fluffy. Step 10: Sandwich the two layers of cake with whipped cream and chopped cherries. Step 11: Top with whipped cream and cherries. Step 12: Sprinkle generously with grated chocolate. Step 13: Chill and serve.
